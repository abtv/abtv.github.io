Architecture decision record

The problem

You want to make a significant change which will affect other team members.

Solution

What to include in ADR

  1. Shortly describe decision itself.
  2. Explain why we made the decision. The problem and tradeoffs are most important.

For big changes

Create an RFC and describe your change and why we need to do it. Then start discussion and assess your change by your coallegues. When we have a conclusion write an ADR.

For small changes

Just write an ADR without RFC.

Why?

This will help to onboard new team members. And the team members will always understand why we made this decision.
