Rationality

Rationality is an ability to achieve goal with minimum efforts.

It is clear you need:

  1. To know exactly what you want.
  2. Be honest with yourself: your mental model should be aligned with reality.
  3. Resources.

Where to start if you see an impossible problem

Sometimes we think it's impossible to do something, let's skip it.

One thing may help: set a 10 minutes timer and think about the problem like you have all necessary resources.

If after 10 minutes you still think you are at the same place and don't see any clues, ok, skip it.
