Discipline

Benefits of discipline

You will save efforts

When you do something regularly it's much easier to start doing it.

Discipline will give you a special pleasure

The pleasure goes from doing things regularly.

If you are an anxious person discipline will give you more stability

Find a comfort environment, create a schedule and do something relevant to you regulalry.

How to start

  • go in small steps: you will feel discomfort but it's bearable;
  • find a comfort environment;
  • create a routine: do it every day (every several days) at the same time;
  • if you feel you need to skip your routine today you should NOT do it except you are sick or you have a VERY urgent need.
