Break the ice

Ask open questions: let other people to talk.

Examples of the questions

  • How’s your week going?
  • What is your favourite job-related thing?
  • What is your least favoured job-related thing?
  • What was the last thing that the team shipped that you were really proud of?
  • How are your teammates doing?
  • What’s the one thing that nobody wants to speak up about?
  • What’s the one thing that you think I could help you with?
