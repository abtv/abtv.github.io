Boredom vs brain overstimulation

The problem

Looks like human brain either consumes new information or generates new ideas. If you feel overwhelmed your brain will struggle generating new ideas.

Solution

Try regularly stop consuming interesting information. Take a day off (more is better) of the Internet or smartphone. You should feel bored and after some time your brain will start producing new ideas.
