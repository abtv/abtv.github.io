SQ3R

This is an algorithm which helps to read difficult texts by keeping your focus on the topic.

It consists of the following steps:

  • (S)urvey - quickly scan through text.
  • Write down (Q)uestions - based on your quick scan from the previous step.
  • (R)ead text trying to answer your questions.
  • (R)ecall text: remember and say aloud (or in your head) the most important parts.
  • (R)eview text: almost the same as recall but done after some time.

How I use it

I quickly scan table of contents or a separate chapter and if I feel an interest in some particular sentence, table or diagram I make a note either by writing down or in my head (only if it's something so extraordinary so I can't forget about it). When I finish scanning I start to read only relevant parts of a book or an article.
