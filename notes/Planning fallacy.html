Planning fallacy

The problem

People rather tend to underestimate cost and overestimate outcomes of projects.

The solution

  1. Write down your idea of a project and go to do something else.
  2. In a day review your project and think if you still want to work on the project.
  3. Try to be pessimistic about an expected outcome.
  4. Write down necessary steps to implement your project and add extra cost to unclear steps.
