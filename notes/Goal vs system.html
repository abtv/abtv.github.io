Goal vs system

Goal

Goal is an outcome in the future. I have NO full control over it.

System

System is what I use and experience every day toward a goal. I DO have full control over it.

What to choose

I need both. If a goal is not relevant to me or a process is not pleasant or doesn't drive me I will be unhappy.
