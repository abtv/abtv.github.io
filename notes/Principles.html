Principles

1. Solve other people’s problems

  • Try to be a solution, not a problem
  • Walk in your customer’s shoes

2. Listen actively

Understand why other people do what they do

3. Make notes

See signal and ignore noise. Write down important things.

4. Be boring

  • Discipline is a useful thing
  • Overtime is destructive in a long term
