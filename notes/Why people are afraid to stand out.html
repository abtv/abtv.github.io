Why people are afraid to stand out

Hypothesis

Zebras have a camuflage that makes difficult lions to attack the same zebra (usually more than one lion is needed to successfully kill a zebra and it means lions must locate their target). If a zebra has something that allows to distinguish it from its herd lions can easily coordinate to hunt the zebra.

Looks like people have a similar built-in mechanism which makes us uncomfortable to stand out of others.
