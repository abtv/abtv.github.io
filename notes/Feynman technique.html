Feynman technique

The learning technique

If you want truly understand some difficult topic try to explain it in such simple words so it would understand a 6-7 year old child.

Important details

  1. Use analogies, it's helpful.
  2. If you see that you can't explain something you need to stop explaining and try to figure out what you don't know. Then return to your explanation.
