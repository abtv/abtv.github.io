English upper-intermediate notes

Here are my notes from an English course I am going through. Each header is a separate topic.

Work at startup

  • get settled in - adapt to
  • catch on - understand
  • I was feeling - long but finished action
  • I really wasn't expecting - unusual situation
  • join in on
  • get them up and running - to make them working
  • give them some direction on - to show what's important
  • I'm going to ask you to - polite way to order to do something
  • leave a company (not quit)
  • they used a strange vocabulary (not words)
  • hold your hand - help you

Studying at university

  • get expelled
  • get suspneded
  • kicked out of (school, etc)
  • keep off (the streets, etc)
  • deception
  • integrity
  • breakdown
  • be together (she seems so together) - to be confident, organized and emotionally stable.
  • I didn't mean to pry
  • he has problems waking up
  • if I didn't sleep well

Traumas from the past

  • snap out of it
  • let yourself go
  • burn the candle at both ends
  • screw someone over
  • be out of it

Facing fears

  • take a step
  • last resort
  • stay the course
  • open up
  • pull yourself out of a rut
  • confront something head-on

The most boring place

  • urban decay
  • newsworthy
  • hot tip
  • scoop
  • dramatic landscape
  • my wife and me ... (in this order)
  • me and my friends ... (in this order)

Dancing mania

  • famine
  • urge
  • craze
  • get caught up in something
  • can't help yourself
  • luckily no one was hurt
  • my car spun around into the opposite direction

Miscellaneous

  • couch potato: physically inactive person.
