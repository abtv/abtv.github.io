English

Using English at work may be the best way to improve your English. There are other ways, especially if you want to learn a lot of new words and phrases.

How to train writing

Write a blog. Like this one.

How to learn new words

  1. Read news in English: Euronews and Guardian.
  2. Use English vocabulary in use advanced →: read it and do exercises. It will take 3 months if you do one unit per day.

How to train speaking and listening

  1. Create a habit of listening Apple News Today.
  2. Find a native speaker on Italki. Practice at least 2 times per week, one hour at a time.
  3. Listen to Scrubs tv series with subtitles and write down new words and phrases.
