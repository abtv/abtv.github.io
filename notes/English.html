How to learn new words

  1. Read news in English. I started reading Euronews and Guardian and then started to explore more news sites. It's important to read non-technical topics: you will learn more new words and phrases.
  2. Take an intermediate, upper-intermediate, or advanced course of English and do all the exercises. How to train speaking and listening

  1. I usually search "news" on YouTube in the morning and watch something interesting while I'm making my breakfast.
  2. Find a native speaker on Italki or Preply. Practice at least 2 times per week, one hour at a time. This is important for upper-intermediate level and above.
  3. Watch one episode of Scrubs, Morning Show, any other interesting TV-series without subtitles. Switch movies from time to time because it's necessary to learn new accents to improve listening skill.
