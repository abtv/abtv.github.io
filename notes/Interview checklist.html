Interview checklist

The checklist

1. Remove my video from my screen: it will help me to focus on another person.

2. Questions for a recruiter

  • What are the working hours? How often the team works overtime?
  • How many days of PTO per year will I have?
  • Do I need to be on a call (SRE)? If yes how frequently?
  • Do you have a health insurance paid by the company? (for non-startups)
  • Do you provide options? (for startups)
  • If they ask for my salary expectations give them a range because it depends on the factors above.

3. Questions for my potential manager

  • Who will I work close to day to day? Is it only one or several teams?
  • Which experience the team(s) has? What can I learn from the team?
  • How company is going to onboard me?
  • Could you describe my ideal working day?
  • What is expected from me? How will I know that I'm doing my job well?
  • Which engineering practices (design review, continuous delivery, automated logs and alerts, etc) does the company use?
